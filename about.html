Our Story

Welcome to The Outer Clove, a culinary haven nestled in the heart of Colombo and spanning its flavors across many vibrant cities. Since our inception in 1990, The Outer Clove has been a beacon of exquisite dining experiences, weaving together tradition, innovation, and a commitment to excellence. Our story begins with a vision to create a space where food becomes an art form, a celebration of flavors that transport you to a realm of gastronomic delight. Over the years, we have evolved from a humble establishment to a culinary destination that captures the essence of diverse cuisines while maintaining a deep-rooted connection to our roots. At The Outer Clove, every dish tells a story, a narrative of passion and dedication that has been woven into the fabric of our culinary journey. Our chefs, inspired by a myriad of global influences, craft each menu with precision and creativity, ensuring an unforgettable dining experience for our patrons. With a commitment to quality and authenticity, we have expanded our footprint beyond Colombo, reaching out to many cities where discerning food enthusiasts can savor the distinct flavors that define The Outer Clove. Our restaurants serve as a testament to our dedication to providing a remarkable fusion of taste, ambiance, and service. As we continue to evolve and innovate, we invite you to join us on this gastronomic adventure. Whether you are a connoisseur of fine dining or seeking a place to create lasting memories, The Outer Clove is where culinary excellence meets warm hospitality. Thank you for being a part of our journey—we look forward to serving you with passion and flavor.
